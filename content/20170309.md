##Hyperworks2內容：

<b>The 2017 HyperWorks release has a lot</b>
<p>在2017年HyperWorks發行很多版本</p>
<b>of enhancements to the modeling and assembly capabilities</b>
<p>對軟件的建模和組裝能力的增強</p>
<b>So we put in a new parts browser with a built-in part library</b>
<p>所以我們在一個新的零件瀏覽器與一個內置零件庫</p>
<b>The part library is a great tool for storing and loading work in progress</b>
<p>零件庫是用於存儲和加載正在進行工作的一個很好的工具</p>
<b>So you can store a catalog of all the common parts</b>
<p>因此，您可以存儲所有公共部分的目錄</p>
<b>that a workgroup needs to use,so they can download their models from their PLM system</b>
<p>工作組需要使用，因此他們可以從他們的PLM系統下載他們的模型</p>
<b>and store them locally in a parts library for a work in progress</b>
<p>並將它們本地存儲在正在進行的工作的零件庫中 </p>
<b>And it keeps revision control on them,</b>
<p>他保持對它們的修訂控制</p>
<b>so they can back up to the different versions and reload.</b>
<p>所以他們可以備份到不同的版本並重新加載。</p>
<b>And they can also build different configurations now of their models.</b>
<p>他們也可以建立不同的配置到他們現在的的模型。</p>
<b>In the configuration modeler now</b>
<p>現在在配置建模器</p>
<b>they can import parts from part library and then group</b>
<p>他們可以從零件庫導入零件然後分組</p>
<b>them into what we call part sets,which are</b>
<p>他們變成我們所說的部分集</p>
<b>convenient groupings of parts.</b>
<p>即零件方便分組</p>
<b>And then they can drag those parts together into assemblies,</b>
<p>然後他們可以將這些部分一起拖入組件</p>
<b>and configure them for different load</b>
<p>並為不同負載配置它們的</p>
<b>cases or different variations of their models.</b>
<p>情況或他們的模型的不同變化</p>
<b>the other feature of haveing parts in the software</b>
<p>硬件部分的軟件中的另一個特點</p>
<b>is we do part instancing now,too.</b>
<p>我們現在也做實例化</p>
<b>So if you load in a part that has</b>
<p>所以如果你加載在一個部分</p>
<b>multiple instances through the model,we support that.</b>
<p>我們支持多個實例通過模型</p>
<b>We don't have to create multiple copies of it.</b>
<p>我們不必創建它的多個副本</p>
<b>We can actually do the instancing,which is very memory efficient.</b>
<p>我們可以做實際，這是非常有效的記憶。</p>
<b>Also in the model building assembly</b>
<p>也在模型建築組裝</p>
<b>tool set is enhanced ID management as well.</b>
<p>工具集也是增強的ID管理</p>
<b>So not only can we assign IDs based on different rules</b>
<p>因此，我們不僅可以根據不同的規則分配ID </p>
<b>that you define in the software,but you can import ID systems</b>
<p>您可以在軟件中定義，但可以導入ID系統</p>
<b>form spreadsheets,XML files,databases,</b>
<p>形式電子表格，XML文件，數據庫，</p>
<b>and so forth,that are then used when</b>
 <p>等等，然後在使用</p>
<b>it does the renumbering or the assembly of the model.</b>
<p>它進行重新編號或模型的組裝</p>
<b>it has to resolve part collisions.</b>
<p>它必須解決部分碰撞。</p>
<b>Really important part of doing model assembly</b>
<p>做模型組裝重要的部分</p>
<b>is connecting all those parts together.</b>
<p>將所有這些部分連接在一起</p>
<b>Connectors are really vital to the overall assembly process.</b>
<p>連接器對整個裝配過程至關重要。</p>
<b>The quality of the connector that you create really</b>
<p>您真正創建的連接器的質量</p>
<b>determines the outcome of the quality</b>
<p>確定質量的結果</p>
<b>of the overall assembled model as well.</b>
<p>對整體組裝模型</p>
<b>So for us to get a good high fidelity solution,</b>
<p>所以為我們得到一個良好的高保真度解決方案</p>
<b>you have to have a really good connection generation as well.</b>
<p>你必須有一個非常好的連接生成</p>
